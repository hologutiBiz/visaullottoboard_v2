This page explains how users who signed in to the Lotto Forecast App using their Facebook account can request the deletion of their data from our systems. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will honour all valid deletion requests promptly.
01

What Data We Collect via Facebook

When you sign in to the Lotto Forecast App using Facebook Login, we collect only the minimum information required to create and authenticate your account:

Data Type Purpose Stored By
Email address Account identification and communication Firebase Authentication (Google Cloud)
Display name Personalizing your in-app experience Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud)
Facebook User ID Linking your Facebook account to your app account Firebase Authentication (Google Cloud)

🔒 We do not access your Facebook friends list, post to your timeline, or store any other Facebook data beyond what is listed above.

02

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all data associated with your Facebook-linked account through either of the following methods:

Method A — Delete Your Account In-App

This is the fastest way to remove all your data immediately:

1
Open the Lotto Forecast App

Log in to your account at app.visuallottoboard.com or via the Android app

2
Go to Account Settings

Tap your profile icon and navigate to Settings → Account

3
Select "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete My Account

4
Confirm Deletion

Confirm your request. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Method B — Submit a Deletion Request by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion by contacting us directly:

1
Send an email to our privacy team

Email: support@visuallottoboard.com

2
Use the subject line

"Facebook Data Deletion Request — [Your Registered Email Address]"

3
Include in your message

Your registered email address and a brief confirmation that you wish to have all your data permanently deleted

4
We will process your request

We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days

Method C — Remove App Access from Facebook

You can also revoke our app's access to your Facebook account directly through Facebook:

1
Go to your Facebook Settings

Visit facebook.com/settings → Apps and Websites

2
Find "Visual Lotto Board" or "Lotto Forecast App"

Locate our app in your connected apps list

3
Click "Remove" and check "Delete posts"

This revokes our access. To ensure full data deletion from our servers, also send us an email using Method B above

03

What Happens After Deletion

Once your deletion request is processed:

  • Your account credentials and profile data will be permanently removed from Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore
  • Your saved preferences, subscription records, and usage history will be deleted
  • Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for statistical improvement purposes only
  • You will lose access to the Lotto Forecast App and any active subscription — no refunds are issued for the remaining subscription period
  • Deletion is irreversible. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the Service again

🕒 All personal data will be fully deleted within 30 days of a confirmed request. You will receive an email confirmation once the deletion is complete.
