App-Specific Information

Android Application

The Lotto Forecast App is currently available on Android. The following device permissions may be requested:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch live draw results, predictions, and app data
  • Notifications: Required to send draw result alerts and subscription reminders
  • Storage: Used for local data caching to improve app performance

Google Play Services may independently collect device identifiers and app performance metrics in accordance with Google's own privacy policy.

Web Application

The Lotto Forecast App is also accessible via web browser at app.visuallottoboard.com. Standard browser cookies and session data are used to maintain your login state and preferences.

Forecast Central Panel

The Forecast Central Panel is a dedicated dashboard interface providing an integrated view of the Lotto Classification Chart, Baba Ijebu Game Results, and the current Day Result. This panel is accessible only on large-screen devices (desktop and tablet). No additional personal data is collected through this interface beyond standard usage analytics.

Facebook Login

When you sign in with Facebook, we collect your public profile information (name, profile picture), email address, and Facebook user ID solely for authentication purposes. We do not post to your timeline, access your friend list, or use this data for any purpose other than account identification.
